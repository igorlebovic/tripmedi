About Us

About TripMedi

TripMedi is organizing the clutter in the medical tourism industry by making it easier to research and book world-class medical treatment at affordable prices. Unveiled at the 2011 South by Southwest Conference, TripMedi aggregates all available information about medical tourism – provider reputation, quality of facility, pricing, location, associated services – and presents options in a simple and intuitive way.

How is TripMedi different?

There are many medical tourism sources online – there is something for those seeking affordable, safe and high quality medical care. The problem is that it is difficult to understand and compare information across providers. Many providers are opaque. TripMedi is transparent and trustworthy. Others are highly facilitated providers which work with only a handful of hospitals around the world. TripMedi aggregates information from a large range of suitable care options . We fundamentally believe in delivering an elegant and simple user experience while supplying users with the tools to rate providers and document their experiences.
