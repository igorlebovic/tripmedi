What is medical tourism?

Medical tourism means crossing the border to obtain medical care. It is estimated that between 3 and 5 million people did this in 2010... Read more »

Is it safe and reliable?

TripMedi uses three methods to establish the reputation of the provider:

  • statistical data about number of procedures done every year, staff ratios, age of facilities, etc.
  • Accreditation indexes (JCI, IHC)
  • Recommendations from others

Why should I travel to get healthcare?

The same or better quality treatment is available abroad. It typically costs less than half of what you would pay in the US – in some cases as low as 10%. Many destinations offer a 5 star recovery experience as well. Read more »

How is TripMedi different than medical tourism agencies?

There are many medical tourism sites online – there is something for everybody. The problem is that it is really difficult to understand and compare information across providers.

Some are opaque and shady. We are transparent and trustworthy.

Others are highly facilitated providers which trust only a handful of hospitals around the world – we open up a large range of suitable care options (like Kayak) Read more »
