What is medical tourism?

Medical tourism (also called medical travel, health tourism or global healthcare) is a term initially coined by travel agencies and the mass media to describe the rapidly-growing practice of traveling across international borders to obtain heath care. Read more »

Is it safe and reliable?

Just because something is more affordable doesn’t mean that it is lower quality and less safe. There are hundreds of international hospitals throughout the globe that have been accredited by Joint International Commission, which drives standards and evaluation methods designed to provide quantifiable benchmarks for patient care quality and safety. In fact, many international hospitals today seek accreditation as a way to attract international patients. Read more »

Why should I travel to get healthcare?

The same or better quality treatment is available abroad. It typically costs less than half of what you would pay in the US – in some cases as low as 10%. Many destinations offer a 5 star recovery experience as well. Read more »

How is TripMedi different than medical tourism agencies?

There are many medical tourism sites online – there is something for everybody. The problem is that it is really difficult to understand and compare information across providers.
